About Jeevaa Group

Jeevaa Group is a trusted leader in rendering technology and poultry by-product processing, delivering reliable solutions across India.

Who We Are

Established in 2010, Jeevaa Group provides complete solutions for rendering plants and feed supplements. We are among the largest rendering machinery manufacturers in South India, with more than 27 plants installed across the country.

Our Strength

  • In-house Research & Development
  • High-tech rendering process
  • Customized plant solutions
  • Energy-efficient systems
  • Strong after-sales support

Jeevaa Techmax

Jeevaa Promax
